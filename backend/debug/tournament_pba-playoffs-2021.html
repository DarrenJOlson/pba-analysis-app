PBA competitors will compete in a series of 2-game bracket elimination matches leading up to a best-of-five-games final round to determine the Kia PBA Playoffs champion. Ties will be broken by a 9th and 10th frame roll-off. If players are still tied after a 9th and 10 frame roll-off, the PBA one ball roll-off rule will be used to break the tie. Higher seeds will have bowling order and lane choice in all cases of ties.

Kia PBA Playoffs Prize Fund: $288,000.

The 2021 Kia PBA Playoffs is an invitational event made up of the top 16 PBA players of the 2021 Guaranteed Rate PBA Tour. The 16 players are invited based on 2021 points accumulated during the PBA Players Championship, Kia PBA Tournament of Champions, the PBA World Series of Bowling (five events), the USBC Masters and U.S. Open.
