PBA Tournament of Champions Prize Fund: $500,000.

Entries open online for PBA members January 15, 2021. PBA members must enter online. Phone and mailed entries will not be accepted.

Anyone needing instructions on how to enter online can contact membership services at (206) 332-9688.

PBA50 Tour and PBA Regional Tour champions will enter the PBA Tournament of Champions PTQ.

PBA Tour Champions will enter the PBA Tournament of Champions Elite Field.

Entry priority will be given in the following order, provided PBA Tour champions, regardless of status, have entered twenty (20) days prior to the official start date of the PBA Tournament of Champions event.

  • PBA Tour Champions with the most PBA Tour titles
  • The most recent PBA Tour champions
  • All PBA Regional Tour champions will need to enter the PTQ.

NOTE: We will advance a minimum of 6 from the TOC PTQ.  If the TOC Elite Field doesn't reach 58, we will increase the number advancing from the PTQ to fill a field of 64.

Eligibility
Participant must be a PBA champion.
Participant must hold Full PBA Membership to enter.
Participant must be a USBC member in good standing.
