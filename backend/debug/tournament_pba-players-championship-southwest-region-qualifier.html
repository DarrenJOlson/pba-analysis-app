The PBA Players Championship is open to all PBA members.

Qualifying Rounds

Qualifying rounds are happening in five regions in the U.S. January 14-16, 2022. Before entering check your specified region listed on each tournament page to make sure you are entering the correct regional qualifier. 

Maximum Field Sizes

Field sizes in each region are limited to 96, except for the West which has a limited field of 76.

Entry Priority

Entry priority will be given in the following order, provided PBA members, regardless of status, have entered by the appropriate entry deadline dates listed below.

  • The top 75 point leaders on the 2021 PBA Tour Point List
  • PBA Tour title holders
  • General PBA membership
  • Collegiate bowlers (if spots remain available after the entry deadline date)

Priority Entries (2021 PBA Tour Point Leaders & PBA Tour Title Holders)

Priority entries open online November 1, 2021 at 10 a.m. CST. Spots will be held for priority entrants until November 21,2021 at 11:59 p.m. CST.

General PBA Membership Entries

Entries open online for the entire PBA membership November 22, 2021 at 10 a.m. CST. The final deadline for PBA members to enter is January 9, 2022 at 11:59 p.m. CST. Entries will be confirmed based on the time and date of entry. If entries exceed the maximum field size allowed before entries close on December 31, 2021, a PTQ will be held and a minimum of six will advance from the PTQ.

PBA members must enter online. Phone and mailed entries will not be accepted. Anyone needing instructions on how to enter online, or having difficulty entering this event can contact membership services at (206) 332-9688.

Collegiate Entries

Collegiate bowlers may also compete if spots are available in the tournament. They will be placed on standby until one day after the entry deadline date has passed for PBA members. If spots remain they will be moved to the main field according to their time and date of entry.

PTQ if necessary

Six spots in each region are reserved for the Pro Tour Qualifier if necessary (PTQ). Bowlers entering the PTQ will be charged $250. Bowlers advancing from the PTQ to the main field will be charged an additional $250.
