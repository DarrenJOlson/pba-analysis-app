PBA World Series XII of Bowling Prize Fund: $650,000.

The WSOB entry fee includes all PBA Animal Pattern Championships, the PBA World Championship and complimentary doubles team entry.

NOTE: Doubles will be limited entirely to the 96 players who are bowling the PBA World Series of Bowling. Players will need to enter their doubles team prior to the 1st qualifying day.

The PBA World Series of Bowling is open to all PBA members holding full membership.

Entries open online January 30, 2021 at 10 a.m. CST. PBA members must enter online. Phone and mailed entries will not be accepted.

Anyone needing instructions on how to enter online, or having difficulty entering this event can contact membership services at (206) 332-9688.

PBA members who have entry priority will enter the PBA World Series of Bowling elite field. All other PBA members will enter the PBA World Series of Bowling PTQ.

Entry Priority
Entry priority will be given in the following order, provided PBA members, regardless of status, have entered by the appropriate entry deadline dates listed above. Additionally, all PBA members must pay their entry fee when entering the event.

  • The top 75 point leaders on the 2020 PBA Tour Point List
  • PBA Hall of Famers
  • PBA Tour title holders

Maximum Field Size
Field size is limited to 96, with 12 of those spots being reserved for the Pro Tour Qualifier if necessary (PTQ).

NOTE: Entrants will be placed into the tournament based on date and time the entry was received online. 84 spots are being held for priority entrants. All other entrants will bowl the PTQ. If the tournament does not fill by February 28, 2021 at 11:59 p.m. CST, the PTQ will be cancelled and those entrants will be placed in the elite field.

Entry Fees
Priority Entry Fee: $1000
PTQ Entry Fee: $250.
NOTE: Bowlers advancing from the PTQ into the main field will be charged an additional $750. Any entries received after the final entry deadline will incur a late fee.
