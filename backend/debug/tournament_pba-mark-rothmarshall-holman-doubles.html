Priority Entries

Priority entries open January 3, 2022 at 10 a.m. CT. Priority entries close February 20, 2022 at 11:59 CT p.m.

Entry priority will be given in the following order, provided the top 50 2021 PBA Tour point leaders, 20 WSOB international invites, and PBA Tour champions, regardless of status, have entered by February 20. 40 team spots are being held for priority entrants.

  • Teams consisting of two top 50 2021 PBA Tour point leaders
  • Teams consisting of one PBA Tour title holder and one top 50 point leader
  • Teams consisting of two international invites
  • Teams consisting entirely of one international invite and one top 50 point leader, or one international invite and one PBA Tour title holder, or one PBA Tour title holder and one top 50 point leader, or two PBA Tour title holders
  • Teams consisting of a priority entrant and another PBA member

NOTE: We are holding six spots for teams. If the priority list above fills more than 40 spots, some teams who have entered from the priority list may be placed into the PTQ.

PTQ Entries

PTQ Entries open January 3, 2022 at 10 a.m. CT. PTQ entries close February 20, 2022 at 11:50 CT. All PBA member teams who are not classified as priority entrants will need to enter the PTQ. A minimum of six teams will advance from the PTQ, with a maximum number to be determined to fill the elite field of 46 teams.

Phone and mailed entries will not be accepted. Anyone needing instructions on how to enter online can contact membership services at (206) 332-9688.

Eligibility

Participant must hold PBA Membership to enter, unless bowling for a college.

Collegiate players can enter if spots remain after the entries close for PBA members and International invites.

Entry Fees

PBA PTQ Entries $125 per person, $250 per team.

Teams advancing from the PTQ will be charged an additional $125 per person, $250 per team

PBA Priority Entries $500 per team
