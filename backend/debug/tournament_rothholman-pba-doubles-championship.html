Eligibility

Participant must be a PBA member in good standing to enter unless bowling for a college. College players can enter any PBA Tour event, with the exception of the PBA Tournament of Champions and PBA Players Championship, after entries close for PBA members and they have created their PBA account online. To enter, call John Weber at (314) 974-5399 or email [email protected].

Maximum Field

64 Teams

Point Values

Tier 3 Points

Priority Entries

Priority entries open Monday, Nov. 21 and will close Sunday, April 23 at 11 p.m. CT. Priority will be given in the following order:

  • Teams where both players finished in the Top 50 on the 2022 PBA Tour Point List
  • Teams with one player who finished in the Top 50 on the 2022 PBA Tour Point List and one PBA Tour titlist
  • Teams with two PBA Tour titlists
  • Teams with one player who finished in the Top 50 on the 2022 PBA Tour Point List OR a PBA Tour titlist and one PBA member
  • Teams with two PBA members

Phone and mailed entries will not be accepted. Anyone needing instructions on how to enter online can contact membership services at (206) 332-9688.
