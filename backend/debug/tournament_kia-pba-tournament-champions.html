Priority Entries

Priority Entries open online December 15, 2021. Priority entries close February 7, 2022.

Entry priority will be given in the following order, provided PBA Tour champions, regardless of status, have entered by February 7, 2022.

  • PBA Tour Champions with the most PBA Tour titles
  • The most recent PBA Tour champions

PTQ Entries

PTQ Entries open December 15, 2021. PTQ entries close February 7, 2022. PBA Regional Tour & PBA50 Tour champions will need to enter the PTQ.

NOTE: 64 spots are being held for PBA Tour champions. Eight players will advance from the PTQ.

PBA members must enter online. Phone and mailed entries will not be accepted.

Anyone needing instructions on how to enter online can contact membership services at (206) 332-9688.

Eligibility

Participant must be a PBA champion.

Participant must be a PBA member.
