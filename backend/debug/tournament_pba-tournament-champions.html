Priority Entries

Priority entries open Monday, Nov. 21 and will close Saturday, Feb. 25 at 11 p.m. CT. Priority will be given in the following order, provided PBA Tour champions, regardless of status, have entered by February 25, 2023.

  • PBA Tour Champions with the most PBA Tour titles

Phone and mailed entries will not be accepted. Anyone needing instructions on how to enter online can contact membership services at (206) 332-9688.

Any entries received after the final entry deadline will incur a late fee.
