PTQ Lane Pairings

Sunday, April 30

Pre-Tournament Qualifier (PTQ) (eight games; limited to 120 players): 9 a.m.

Minimum of eight (8) advance to the elite field of 128 in the PBA Players Championship  presented by Snickers

A full schedule of the PBA Players Championship presented by Snickers can be found here.

PBA Players Championship presented by Snickers Prize Fund: $400,000 (based on 128 entries)

Tournament Host Center

Bowlero North Brunswick

1 Carolier Lane

North Brunswick Township, NJ 08902

 (732) 846-2424

Participant must be a PBA member in good standing.

Entries

PTQ entries open Monday, Nov. 21 and close Saturday, April 15 at 11 p.m. CT. All PBA members who are not classified as priority entrants will need to enter the PTQ. A minimum of eight players will advance to the elite field, with a maximum number to be determined to fill the elite field of 128. Example: If 75 point leaders and 20 more PBA Tour champions not among the top 50 2022 PBA Tour point leaders enter as priority entrants, 33 would advance from the PTQ, which would fill the elite field at 128.

Phone and mailed entries will not be accepted. Anyone needing instructions on how to enter online can contact membership services at (206) 332-9688.

Any entries received after the final entry deadline will incur a late fee.

Entry Fees

PBA PTQ Entries $250 (bowlers advancing from the PTQ will be charged an additional $250)
