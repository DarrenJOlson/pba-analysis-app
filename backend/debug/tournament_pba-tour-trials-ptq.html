Priority Entries

Priority 1 entries for PBA Tour players ranked 44-100 open Monday, May 6, 2024. Entries for all other priority levels open Monday, June 3, 2024. All entries close Sunday, June 30 at which time we will confirm final roster for the main field beginning July 17.

PRIORITY LEVELS 2-6 will initially enter in the PTQ. Players will be moved to the main field based on priority level and date/time of entry by PBA Administrators.

Entry priority will be given in the following order for the 80 roster positions

  • Top 100 on 2024 PBA Tour, who are not in top 43 points or with a national title in last 10 years.
  • Top 10 in each PBA region (2023 Points)
  • Top 25 players (from singles rankings) exiting college for PBA membership
  • New international players
  • Top 25 in each PBA region (2023 Points)
  • Any full PBA member (prioritized by date/time of entry)

Remaining 10 roster positions will be filled from the PBA Tour Trials PTQ. Once 80 main field roster positions are full, remaining entries will be moved to the PTQ roster. 

Phone and mailed entries will not be accepted. Anyone needing instructions on how to enter online can contact membership services at (206) 332-9688.

Entry Fees

PBA Tour Trials PTQ: $250 (bowlers advancing from the PTQ will be charged an additional $750)
